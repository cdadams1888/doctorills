Services That We Provide:

Weed Control

Pre-emergent Weed Control:

Pre-emergent Weed Control aims to prevent the young seedlings from sprouting up during the beginning of the season. This service employs the use of carefully timed treatments which stop the seeds from growing during the germination process.

Emergent Weed Control:

Emergent Weed Control addresses the weeds that already exist, and may require both Pre-emergent and Emergent services to provide maximum weed control.

Don't give weeds the chance to see the light of day! Let us come in with weed preventative services to rid this pesky problem for you once and for all!

Insecticide / Fungicide

Insects and fungus are all over the place! They can pester plants & humans alike, not only that but they view your garden and/or landscaping project as a feast! Allow Dr. Ill's to come in and take care of this problem for you.

Fertilize

Lawns that receive fertilization are greener, healthier, and have a greater reduction of weeds. Our early season applications will give your lawn a boost for the upcoming growing season. Dr. Ill's fertilization services will give your lawn the nutrients that it truly needs in order to sucessfully thrive.

Chinch Bugs

Chinch bugs absolutely love the taste of agricultural crops, native grass, and of course your lawn! When Chinch bugs feed they inject a toxin that makes it hard for the plants and grasses to get the water and nutrients that they need from the soil. If you have spots of grass that are yellow or brown, let Dr. Ill's step in for you.

Sod Web Worms

There are many different species of caterpillars called Sod Web Worms which can be highly destructive little pests. They cause the most damage to your grass during the larval stage. This damage usually appears in the early spring, and persists throughout late July and August. If you notice dead or irregular patches that are in direct sunlight, Dr.Ill's can help you with this problem.

Crabgrass

Crabgrass is one of the most missidentified lawn weeds. When Crabgrass grows it can be extremely difficult to control because it drops thousands of seeds onto your lawn. Once Crabgrass takes root it releases toxins that can damage your lawn grass. Dr. Ill's Lawn Management specializes in the abatement of Crabgrass, so let us come take care of it!
